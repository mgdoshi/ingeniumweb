About Ingenium Our flagship product, Equipe, is an advanced Order Tracking System. The system can be configured to cater to requirements that are specific to your organisation. Equipe transcends geographical and time zone barriers, enabling you to communicate, transact and constantly monitor at the click of a button.

With Equipe,  You can :

  • Receive orders from your client/dealers.
  • Monitor pending orders & maintain delivery schedules.
  • Maintain general as well as client-specific price lists.
  • Generate and download various MIS reports.
  • Communicate with your clients/dealers/branches.
     and Your Clients/Dealers can :
  • Access your product/price lists.
  • Place inquiries & orders.
  • Check the status of their orders.
Highlights of Equipe :
  • Extremely user-friendly and secure aplication.
  • No upgrades or additional investment of hardware, software or manpower.
  • Zero implmentation and training time.
  • Fully configurable and customisable according to user specifications.
  • Useful tool for clients, dealers & marketing new products.
